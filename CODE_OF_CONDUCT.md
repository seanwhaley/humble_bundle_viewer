# Code of Conduct

This project aims to be welcoming, respectful, and practical.

## Expected behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent, but listen when impact is pointed out
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and outcomes rather than people
- help keep discussions useful for contributors of different experience levels

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- hateful or discriminatory language
- deliberate publication of private information, credentials, or signed links
- repeated bad-faith disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, lock, or moderate content that violates this code of conduct and may restrict participation for repeat or severe violations.
